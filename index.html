Narek Ohanyan

I'm a PhD candidate at the Department of Economics and Business, Universitat Pompeu Fabra. I hold a MRes in Economics, Finance and Management from Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Master's Degree in Economics and Finance from Barcelona School of Economics.

My research interests are in macroeconomics, monetary economics and time-series econometrics.

The main topics of my current research are asymmetric information in macroeconomic models, financial dollarization, rules versus discretion in monetary policy, etc.
